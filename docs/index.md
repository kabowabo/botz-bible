# AVBotz Electrical Bible
Compiled by Aarav Jain (‘25), Gaurav Gupta (‘26), et al.  
2023–Present  

Welcome to the AVBotz Electrical Bible — the master documentation for all electrical systems of the AVBotz AUVs.  
This guide consolidates circuit schematics, hardware references, pinouts, and safety documentation.
